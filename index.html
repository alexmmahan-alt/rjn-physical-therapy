About Us

A Tradition of Healing Since 1994

RJN Physical Therapy was established in 1994 as a small physical therapy facility. The vision was to create an orthopedic and sports injury private practice, which it has become. Our focus is on quality care for each and every patient. Our therapists and office staff have a genuine interest in serving the needs of our patients.

Our Mission

"We pledge to provide quality, compassionate, and comprehensive care to enable our patients to return to a healthy and active lifestyle."

Popular

Hot & Cold Therapy

Sauna + Cold Plunge — always together

This session combines back-to-back sauna and cold plunge for a powerful contrast therapy protocol. The sauna deeply relaxes muscles, opens blood vessels, and prepares the body — then the cold plunge rapidly constricts circulation, flushing out inflammation and triggering a surge of energy and mental clarity. Together, they create a pumping effect that dramatically accelerates recovery.

Ideal for athletes, post-surgical patients, those managing chronic pain or inflammation, and anyone looking to boost energy and resilience.

Reduces inflammation Faster muscle recovery Boosts circulation Mental clarity & mood

Compression Arm Sleeves

In-clinic compression sleeve sessions

Our compression arm sleeve sessions use graduated compression technology to actively promote blood flow, reduce swelling, and support targeted recovery of the arm and elbow. Applied in-clinic by our team, these sessions are especially beneficial for post-surgical patients, repetitive strain injuries, and athletes recovering from upper extremity overuse.
